ABOUT US

At Seven Keys Bakeshop, we believe that every day deserves a fresh start—and a warm loaf. Nestled in the heart of Lambaguhon, Iligan City, we’re a neighborhood bakery dedicated to serving freshly baked bread, pastries, and sweet treats that bring comfort and joy to every home.

Whether you're picking up your morning pandesal, craving our signature Crema Loaf, or just stopping by for something sweet, we’re here to share a slice of happiness—one bake at a time. We’re proud to be part of the local community, and even prouder to be your go-to for warm bread and friendly service.

Freshly baked. Locally loved. Always from the heart..
