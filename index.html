ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Founded in Iligan City on October 8, 2022, initially as a sole proprietorship before transitioning into a corporation in 2023. It is now a SEC-registered company specializing in real estate sales, rentals, and management, focusing on low-cost raw lots, farm lots, residential, and commercial properties.

In addition, the company also handles service properties such as rights of way, stations, platforms, offices, and parking lots.

ABOUT OUR SERVICES

Our company is proudly diversified, offering services across several key industries. We specialize in real estate services—including buying, selling, leasing, and property management—while also operating a number of other local businesses that serve everyday needs and community development.

Rizen Megacorp offers services like house construction, vehicle rentals, trucking, backfill, backhoe services, and legal services. With plans for expansion across Mindanao and nationwide, the company is committed to client satisfaction and industry growth.
